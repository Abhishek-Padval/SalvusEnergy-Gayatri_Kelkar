Gayatri R Kelkar

Managing Director, Salvus Energy Uk

Gayatri is a distinguished expert and woman entrepreneur in process safety engineering with over 15 years of experience. As the founder of Salvus Energy in 2012, she expanded operations to London in 2022 and currently serves as Managing Director at Salvus Energy UK Ltd. With a Bachelor's in Industrial Chemistry and certifications in functional safety engineering, Gayatri has led numerous risk management studies globally, working on projects spanning India, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, UAE, Morocco, Somalia, Iraq, Uganda, Nigeria, Russia, Uzbekistan, Thailand, Egypt, South America, the UK, and the USA

Salvus Energy, named after the Latin word for "safe," specializes in providing engineering solutions and services to ensure safety and security during all phases of the facility operations in the energy sector. With expertise in Process Safety, Risk Management, Occupational Health and Safety, Fire Protection, Emergency & Crisis Management, and more, Salvus Energy excels in delivering high-quality services to industries such as Oil and Gas, Petrochemical, Chemical, Pharmaceutical, and other energy sectors. Committed to innovation and excellence, Salvus Energy thrives on solving challenging problems in both Greenfield and Brownfield projects while prioritizing health, safety, and environmental considerations in all aspects of their business operations.
